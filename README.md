# Financial News Mining - Headline Sentiment & Stock Price Correlation 



Mining sentiment from financial news headlines and correlating it with stock price direction (DJIA up/down).



## Why this repo?

- Mine news headlines.
- Compute sentiment.
- Correlate with stock price movements.


## Current Notebooks / Content

- `financial_news_sentiment_stock.ipynb` → Headline sentiment scoring + DJIA direction correlation

Uses: TextBlob (simple sentiment), yfinance (stock data)


## Key Open Datasets Used / Planned

Kaggle: Financial News Headlines (2000–2020) + stock prices

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ankurzing/sentiment-analysis-for-financial-news

Or: Combined News DJIA (25 headlines per day + DJIA prices)

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aaron7sun/stocknews
